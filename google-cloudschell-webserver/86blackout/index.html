8.6mm Blackout: Advantages & Disadvantages

Advantages

  • Designed for short barrels (as short as 8").
  • Supports both supersonic and subsonic loads.
  • High rotational energy for maximum terminal effect.
  • Compatible with AR-10 platforms with only a barrel change.
  • Excellent for medium-range hunting and tactical use.

  • Not ideal for long-range accuracy.
